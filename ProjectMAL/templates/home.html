Welcome To Malaria Outbreak Prediction

Malaria is one of the major public health problems in India. Early prediction of a Malaria outbreak is the key for control of malaria morbidity, mortality as well as reducing the risk of transmission of malaria in the community and can help policymakers, health providers, medical officers, ministry of health and other health organizations to better target medical resources to areas of greatest need. Here developed model “Malaria outbreak prediction Model using Machine Learning” can help as an early warning tool to identify potential outbreaks of Malaria.
